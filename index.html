About the Band

Shattered Riffs is a high-energy rock band known for powerful live performances and raw sound. Shattered Riffs doesn't just play rock; they unleash it. With influences ranging from the rawness of 70s punk to the heaviness of modern hard rock, the band creates a wall of sound characterized by distorted guitars, thunderous rhythms, and lyrics that explore rebellion and the urban experience.
